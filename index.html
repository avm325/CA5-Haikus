Autumn leaves fallen
There is beauty in decay
Orange like lanterns

Weather beaten fall
before a frosty winter
betrayed by the leaves

it was yesterday
when old distant me had changed
changed by watching you

Seasons come and go
I return to branches bare
Must I grow again?

When will spring arrive?
Leaves provide no company
The trees stand alone

Time moved so quickly
The semester gone away
What will I become?
