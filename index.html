CHANGE

It was yesterday
When old distant me had changed
Changed by watching you

Autumn leaves fallen
There is beauty in decay
Orange like lanterns

Weather beaten fall
Before a frosty winter
Betrayed by the leaves

Tickling my two ears
A sidewalk ringling of bells
Penny pocket change

Seasons come and go
I return to branches bare
Must I grow again?

When will spring arrive?
Leaves provide no company
The trees stand alone

Time moved so quickly
The semester gone away
What will I become?

Clouds fly overhead
Dark reflection deep puddle
Old rain new mirror

Something is coming
Swept up quick going somewhere
Look I am different

I see a dead leaf
That drags across the pavement
It knows how I feel

Change is difficult
Should I stay or should I go?
Torn between two ways

Change is forever
The only thing that remains
After all is gone

Day and night, tides rise
Wrinkles form, time trails along
Seasons change, we too

An exchange of goods
Bags heavier, wallet light
Change in my pocket
